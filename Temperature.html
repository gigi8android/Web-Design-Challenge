Max Temperature

The graphs depicts that the weather becomes warmer (i.e. higher temperature) as it approaches the equator (0 degree lattitude). The further away from the equator the temperature is lower. Areas in the northern hemisphere (e.g. lattitude 40 to 60 degree) are having lower temperature than the southern hemisphere at their corresponding latitudes (i.e. lattitude -40 to -60 degree). One of the reasons could be the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.

This demonstrates that there is a very strong corelation between the temperature and lattitude.
