Wind Speed

The graph represents wind speed in various areas with varying latitude. It depicts that many cities are in the areas with lattitude ranging from -40 to 70 degrees can experience various wind speed ranging from 0mph to gusty wind above 35mph. There is very weak negative (or near zero r-value) correlation between the wind speed and latitude in northern hemisphere but it gets stronger (i.e. higher negative r-value) in southern hemisphere.

This demonstrates that there is a very weak correlation between the latitude and wind speed.
